PROJECT – FINAL DAY
Asset & Facility Issue Management System
Problem Statement
In a corporate office, employees face multiple facility and IT-related issues such as:
•	Laptop not working
•	Internet issues
•	AC or projector problems
•	Furniture damage
Currently, these issues are reported informally, leading to delays and lack of tracking.
Your task is to design and build a Power Platform application that allows:
•	Employees to raise issues
•	Facility team to track and resolve them
•	Management to monitor issue status
